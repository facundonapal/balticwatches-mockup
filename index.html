Automatic

HMS 001

The HMS 001. is a classy automatic watch inspired from the 1940's step-case watches. This automatic watch is powered by a Miyota 821A in a 38mm 316L stainless steel case with an Italian calf leather strap.
Order the HMS 001

Chronograph

Bicompax 001

The BICOMPAX 001. is a chronograph inspired from the 1940's step-case watches. This mechanical watch is powered by a manual wound ST1901 movement in a 38mm 316L stainless steel case with an Italian calf leather strap.
Order the Bicompax 001
